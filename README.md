### PlasmaRand - Prototype random number generator based on plasma lamps
* **Project management:** Managing a team of students in the construction of an innovative device utilising plasma lamp dynamics as a source of entropy.
* **Statistical validation (RNG Goodness):** Conducting rigorous randomness quality tests in accordance with global cryptographic standards.
